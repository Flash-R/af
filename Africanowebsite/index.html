About us

We are members of the community foundation from Kitagwenda District dedicated to serving our brothers and sisters who are Orphans, Women, Vulnerable children and the Poor, who are unable to sustain themselves by sharing our blessings. We serve one community at a time, contributing to projects in economics, education, and health. We are building relationships with Africano Foundation Uganda. The group shall be a voluntary, non-profit making organization.

More

Aims and Objectives

  • To provide appropriate and better education on both National and Local priorities for the orphans, poor and vulnerable children.
  • To provide guidance and counseling to Children, women and the poor living with HIV/AIDS
  • To mobilize, sensitize and initiate income generating activities.
  • To alleviate poverty and broaden the income base at household level through agricultural activities.
