About us

We are members of the community foundation from Kitagwenda District dedicated to serving our brothers and sisters who are Orphans, Women, Vulnerable children and the Poor, who are unable to sustain themselves by sharing our blessings. We serve one community at a time, contributing to projects in economics, education, and health. We are building relationships with Africano Foundation Uganda. The group shall be a voluntary, non-profit making organization.

our cause

Arinaitwe Africano AFU Founder and Spearhead: When he was doing research as a University student on the rampant increase of HIV/AIDS he found out very many Orphans, women and other Vulnerable Children crying for help. The reason of such sufferings was as a result of the majority who loses their parents and guardians, spouses because of HIV/AIDS.

Aims and objectives

  • To provide appropriate and better education on both National and Local priorities for the orphans, poor and vulnerable children.
  • To mobilize, sensitize and initiate income generating activities.
  • To provide guidance and counseling to Children, women and the poor living with HIV/AIDS.
  • To alleviate poverty and broaden the income base at household level through agricultural activities.
  • To provide knowledge and skills to NGO members and entire community in order to make themselves reliant and self-sustainable.
  • To improve on the sanitation and health services for better standards of the community.
  • To promote and support credit revolving scheme to enable the members who cannot acquire basic needs to enhance household development

our mission

Provide the Orphans and other vulnerable children, the needy women and the poor, with basics health and Education
